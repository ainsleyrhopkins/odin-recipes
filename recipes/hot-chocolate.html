Hot Chocolate

This hot chocolate recipe makes the perfect amount to serve 2 on a chilly winter day. Top with nutmeg or cinnamon and homemade whip cream for best results.

Ingredients

  • 30-40 g chocolate chips
  • 3 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 Tbsp boiling water
  • 1.75 c milk
  • 0.25 tsp vanilla
  • 0.25 c half and half

Steps

  1. Combine cocoa powder, sugar, salt, and boiling water in a small saucepan on medium-high heat.
  2. Bring mixture to a boil, add in chocolate chips, and stir constantly for 2 minutes or until chips are mostly melted.
  3. Stir in milk and heat until the mixture begins to steam. Do not bring to a boil again.
  4. Remove from heat and add in vanilla.
  5. Divide between two large mugs, add 1/8 c. half-and-half to each cup, and enjoy.
